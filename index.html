Shape of Sting's Heart - Electric Cello

Hi, I'm Harry Ellison-Wright and I play the electric cello. It's a fantastic instrument with a unique and underappreciated sound. With your support, I can record my first album and hopefully share some incredible music with you. I have played the cello for eight years and first decided to try and electric version one year ago. I found the results to be amazing as the sounds you can play on an electric instrument are limitless. This debut album will include five original songs and two bonus collaborations. If you back me, you will become a part of this project and will have helped me fulfil this dream.
